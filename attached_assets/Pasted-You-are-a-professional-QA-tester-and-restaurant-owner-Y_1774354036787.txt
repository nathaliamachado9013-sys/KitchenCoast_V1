Test the system in two complete scenarios:

==================================================
SCENARIO 1 — PIZZERIA (SIMPLE OPERATION)
==================================================

Context:
You own a small pizzeria starting from scratch.

Goal:
Validate the system for a focused menu with few ingredients and high repetition.

--------------------------------------------------
Step 1 — Initial Setup
--------------------------------------------------

- Configure currency (EUR)
- Add basic fixed costs (rent, energy, salaries)

- Currency is consistent everywhere

--------------------------------------------------
Step 2 — Import First Invoice
--------------------------------------------------

Simulate a realistic invoice with:

- Flour (25 Kg)
- Tomato sauce (5 L)
- Mozzarella (10 Kg)
- Olive oil (3 L)

Include at least one tricky line:
- "2 caixas de 12 pizzas bases"
- "1/2 Kg fermento"

- AI extraction (if used)
- Manual correction
- Unit conversion
- Review step
- Conversion confirmation UI

- All quantities converted correctly
- No unresolved conversions allowed before import

--------------------------------------------------
Step 3 — Validate Stock
--------------------------------------------------

- All items entered stock

- Cost per unit is correct

--------------------------------------------------
Step 4 — Create Recipe (Pizza Margherita)
--------------------------------------------------

Ingredients:
- Flour
- Tomato sauce
- Mozzarella
- Olive oil

Set:
- quantities
- margin = 70%

Expected:

- cost calculated correctly
- price suggestion generated
- unit conversions applied correctly

--------------------------------------------------
Step 5 — Menu
--------------------------------------------------

- Add pizza to menu
- Check:
  - ideal price
  - actual price
  - "Use suggested price" button

Expected:

- price suggestion visible
- profit calculated correctly

--------------------------------------------------
Step 6 — Sales Simulation
--------------------------------------------------

- Sell 5 pizzas

Expected:

- cost_at_time_of_sale frozen
- profit calculated correctly

--------------------------------------------------
Step 7 — Delete Invoice
--------------------------------------------------

Expected:

- stock returns to previous state
- supplier totals updated
- no orphan data
- no broken state
