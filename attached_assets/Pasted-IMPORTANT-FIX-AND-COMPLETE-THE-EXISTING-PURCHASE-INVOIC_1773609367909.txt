PART 9 — WEIGHTED AVERAGE COST (REQUIRED)
==================================================

Use weighted average cost as the current cost method.

Do NOT use last purchase price as the main cost logic.
Do NOT implement FIFO in this cycle.

Formula:

new_total_value = current_total_stock_value + purchase_line_total
new_total_quantity = current_stock_quantity + purchase_quantity
new_average_cost = new_total_value / new_total_quantity

If current stock was zero before purchase:
new_average_cost = purchase unit cost

Track for each stock item:
- current_stock_quantity
- current_average_cost
- current_total_stock_value

Also record historical cost movement info for each purchase/import:
- date
- quantity added
- unit_price
- line_total
- resulting_average_cost
